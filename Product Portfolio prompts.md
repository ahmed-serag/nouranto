**Context:**
Design a B2B "Product Portfolio" page for Nouranto,The page must serve as a technical reference for international procurement officers to verify seasonal availability and product specifications.

**Visual Direction:**

* **Style:** Data-rich, clean, and clinical. Use a "Modern AgTech" aesthetic.
* **Colors:** Same Colors used in the other pages
* **Components:** Interactive tables, tabbed navigation, and technical specification cards.

**Page Structure & Content:**

**1. Header Section:**

* **Title:** "Export Availability & Technical Specifications"
* **Subtext:** "80 years of precision in Egyptian cultivation. Real-time seasonal windows and cultivar data."

**2. Interactive Crop Calendar Module:**

* **Format:** A horizontal month-by-month (Jan–Dec) matrix table.
* **Rows:**
    * **Table Grapes:** Active status from **May to July**.
    * **Strawberries:** Active status from **November to February**.
    * **Spring Onions:** Active status from **October to April**.
    * **Green Beans:** Active status from **October to July**.
    * **Mange Tout / Sugar Snaps:** Active status from **November to May**.
    * **Iceberg Lettuce:** Active status from **November to May**.

* **UI Feature:** Use color-coded bars to represent "Peak Harvest" vs. "Availability."

**3. Varietal Detail Sections (Technical Cards):**
Create distinct, deep-dive sections with the following data:

* **Category 1: Table Grapes (Early Sweet & Sugraone)**
    * **Scientific Name:** *Vitis vinifera*
    * **Brix Level:** High sweetness profile, ranging from **16° to 20°+**.
    * **Flavor Profile:** Early Sweet features a unique light Muscat flavor; Sugraone is sweet-neutral and crunchy.
    * **Shelf Life:** **45–60 days** with SO2 pads and managed cold chain.
    * **Berry Size:** 16mm – 22mm+.

* **Category 2: Strawberries (Fortuna)**
    * **Scientific Name:** *Fragaria x ananassa*
    * **Brix Level:** **12°+**; high sweetness with a vibrant, deep red internal color.
    * **Characteristics:** Known for early fruit onset and high productivity.
    * **Shelf Life:** **7–10 days** under strict temperature control (0°C).
    * **Packaging:** 2.5kg cartons (10x250g punnets).

* **Category 3: Peas (Mange Tout & Sugar Snaps)**
    * **Scientific Name:** *Pisum sativum var. macrocarpon*
    * **Brix Level:** Characterized by high natural sugars and fiberless, tender pods.
    * **Shelf Life:** **7–10 days** from harvest to retail.
    * **Cold Chain:** Must be stored as close to **0°C** as possible to maintain tenderness.

* **Category 4: Green Beans (Fine & Extra Fine)**
    * **Scientific Name:** *Phaseolus vulgaris*
    * **Characteristics:** Straight, stringless, and dark green.
    * **Sizing:** Fine (6-9mm), Extra Fine (<6mm).
    * **Shelf Life:** **7–10 days** at 4-7°C.
    * **Harvest Window:** **October – July**.

* **Category 5: Spring Onions (Photon & Giza Large)**
    * **Scientific Name:** *Allium cepa*
    * **Profile:** Mild pungency, crisp texture, and pure white bulbs.
    * **Preparation:** Washed, peeled, and root-trimmed.
    * **Shelf Life:** **14–21 days** at 0°C.
    * **Harvest Window:** **October – April**.

* **Category 6: Iceberg Lettuce**
    * **Scientific Name:** *Lactuca sativa*
    * **Weight:** 400g – 600g per head.
    * **Profile:** Dense, crisp heads with excellent shelf stability.
    * **Shelf Life:** **14–21 days** at 0-1°C.
    * **Harvest Window:** **November – May**.

**4. Quality Assurance Footer:**

* A small section reminding users of certifications: **BRC Grade A, Global GAP, and SMETA/SEDEX** compliance for all listed products.

**Design Constraints:**

* Use a clean grid layout for the Varietal Detail sections.
* Ensure the Crop Calendar is mobile-responsive (horizontal scroll on small screens).
* Add a "Download Full Technical PDF" button at the bottom of the page.

**Output Expectation:**
Generate a structured web UI with high-readability tables and clean technical cards. Provide the HTML and Tailwind CSS code.
