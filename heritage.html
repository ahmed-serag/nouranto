Generations of Growth.

Our journey from local growers to international exporters was forged through resilience. Founded by Khaled El Meligy in 1988, Nouranto is more than a business—it's a family promise. Today, Ismail and Nouran El Meligy continue this legacy, blending deep-rooted values with modern innovation to serve our European partners with unwavering accountability.

Our Commitment

Clean Agriculture &
Global Food Security.

At Nouranto, our mission extends far beyond the bottom line. We believe that true leadership in agriculture means taking responsibility for the planet and the people we feed.

Clean Agriculture: We are dedicated to sustainable farming practices that minimize environmental impact, optimize water usage, and ensure the health of our soil.

Food Security: By maintaining a secure, controlled supply chain, we contribute to the stability of the global food system, ensuring that safe, nutritious produce reaches tables around the world, regardless of market volatility.
