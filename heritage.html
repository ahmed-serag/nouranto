The Legacy of
Khaled El Meligy.

Founded by Khaled El Meligy in 1988, Nouranto began as a family business rooted in the fertile lands of Egypt. For over three decades, Khaled led the company with a unique business philosophy that prioritized integrity, reliability, and excellence.

His leadership transformed Nouranto from local growers into a reputable family business and fully accredited global exporter. Though we mourn his passing at the end of 2025, his legacy remains the heartbeat of our company, engraved in every person and part of our Nouranto family.

Khaled’s unique business philosophy remains our North Star, guiding our unwavering commitment to top international standards and the mutual trust we’ve built with our partners over the years.

Ismail and Nouran El Meligy represent the next generation of Nouranto’s leadership. Having gained extensive and rich experience over the years by working in various areas of the business, they have absorbed Khaled’s unique business philosophy.

Our Commitment

Sustainability &
Food Safety.

At Nouranto, we believe that taking challenges has always been the key to our success, and innovation is the key to our future. True leadership in agriculture means honoring our 70-year heritage while delivering unwavering reliability to our international partners.

Sustainability: A core part of our mission is to ensure a sustainable supply and growth through continuous research and innovation in agricultural methodologies. We prioritize environmental responsibility by using R-PET and recycling materials in our packaging, minimizing our ecological footprint.

Food Safety: We are committed to transforming cultivation into a secure, accredited export process—mitigating market volatility through controlled chains and strict adherence to international safety standards including Global GAP, BRC, and SMETA.
