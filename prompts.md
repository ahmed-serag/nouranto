Context: Design a premium B2B homepage for "Nouranto," an Egyptian agricultural export company with an 80-year heritage. The target users are international retail procurement officers and supermarket category managers from the UK and EU.

Visual Direction:

Vibe: High-trust, professional, and legacy-focused.

Color Palette: Clean whites, deep forest greens (representing growth), and soft earth tones.

Typography: Elegant serif headings for a "heritage" feel, with clean sans-serif body text for readability.

Aesthetics: Modern "Bold Minimalism" with high-resolution imagery of Egyptian farmland and fresh produce.

Layout & Section Hierarchy:

Header:

Sticky navigation with the Nouranto logo.

Menu: Home, Our Heritage, Farms, Packhouse, Products, Contact.
