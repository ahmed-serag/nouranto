Varieties & Characteristics

Our green pea varieties are selected for their delicate sweetness and crisp texture. We specialize in edible-pod peas which are harvested young to ensure they are fiberless and tender.

  • check_circle
    Snow Peas (Mange Tout) Flat pods with small, premature seeds. Consumed whole.
  • check_circle
    Sugar Snap Peas Plump, rounded edible pods with thick walls. Sweet and crunchy.
  • check_circle
    Garden Peas Traditional shelling peas, sweet and tender.

Export Standards

All our Green Peas are Global GAP certified. We ensure strict cold chain management from the moment of harvest to preserve the natural sugar content (brix) and prevent starch conversion.
